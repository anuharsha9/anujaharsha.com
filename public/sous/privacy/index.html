Privacy Policy

Effective June 28, 2026

Sous is a hands-free cooking app by Anuja Harsha Nimmagadda. The short version: Sous has no account and no server of its own, collects nothing about you, and keeps your data on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Sous has no accounts, no sign-up, no analytics, no advertising, and no servers that receive your data. We don’t build a profile on you, and we never sell or share your information — because we never have it.

What stays on your device

Your recipes, grocery and pantry lists, tastes and preferences, cooking history, and your AI API key (if you add one) are stored only in Sous’s storage on your device. They are not uploaded to us. Deleting the app — or using Profile → Privacy → Clear everything — removes all of it immediately.

Bring-your-own-key AI (optional)

Sous works fully without any AI key: its 300+ recipes, cook-along guidance, timers, and nutrition run on your device. If you turn on Live AI, you provide your own API key from Anthropic (Claude) or Google (Gemini). In that case:

• Your key is stored only on your device and is never sent to us or logged by us.
• When you use a live feature, the request (for example, the recipe text you’re importing or the message you typed) is sent directly from your device to the AI provider you chose, using your key. There is no server of ours in between.
• What the provider does with that request is governed by their privacy policy: Anthropic · Google.

Microphone & voice

If you use hands-free voice, Sous needs microphone access. Your spoken audio is streamed directly to Google’s Gemini Live service using your own key, and only while a voice session is active, so Sous can respond. We do not record, store, or receive your audio. You can deny or revoke microphone access in iOS Settings at any time; the rest of the app still works.

Images

Recipe photos are matched from public sources on the web. No personal data is involved.

Children

Sous is rated 4+ and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Data sharing & sale

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone. There is nothing to share.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we’ll update the effective date above and post the new version at this URL. Material changes will be reflected in an app update.

Contact

Questions? Use Profile → Feedback inside the app, or email feedback@anujaharsha.com.
